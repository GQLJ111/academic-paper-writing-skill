## Useful English Sentence Forms

- “This study investigates whether and how ...”
- “To isolate ..., we control ...”
- “The comparison suggests that ... rather than ...”
- “The effect is scenario-dependent: ...”
- “This result should be interpreted as ..., not as ...”
- “A possible explanation is that ...”
- “These findings extend prior work by ...”

## Useful Chinese Sentence Forms

- “为避免将……简单归因于……，本文进一步区分……”
- “在控制……的条件下，……主要反映……的影响。”
- “这一结果说明，……并非简单随……线性变化。”
- “需要注意的是，该结论适用于……，不应直接推广到……”
- “与仅报告……不同，本文同时考察……”
